Help

Quick guide to the Smart City dashboard.

  • Login: Start at the login page, use the prefilled credentials, and press Login to enter.
  • Navigation: Use the top bar links (Dashboard, Settings, Help) to move around.
  • Profile menu: Click the avatar in the top right to view status or sign out (returns to login).
  • Live Map: View incidents and recenter with “Center on Patras”; refresh with “Refresh now”.
  • Analytics: The Grafana panel shows traffic metrics; use “Refresh” to reload the iframe.
  • AI Assistant: Type a prompt in the AI card and press “Send” for LLM responses.
